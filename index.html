ItaLean 2025

Bridging Formal Mathematics and AI

Bologna, Italy – | December 9-12, 2025

Supported by

ItaLean 2025 brings together researchers and practitioners advancing the formalization of mathematics in Lean and exploring the interplay between machine learning and formal methods. The program includes invited lectures, hands-on tutorials, research talks from academia and industry, product demos, and a closing panel discussion.

Participate

Applications to participate in ItaLean 2025 are now open. Priority will be given to applications submitted by October 31.

Participation is free of charge and includes access to all conference sessions. A limited amount of financial support for travel and accommodation may be available. Applicants may indicate their need for support in the application form.

Applicants are encouraged to include a CV with their submission.
